Privacy

Our privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We are committed to maintaining your trust by ensuring your data is handled responsibly and securely at all times.

Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide better services and improve your experience on our platform. This includes personal details you provide and data collected automatically when you interact with our website.

Personal Information

  • Your name, email address, and contact information
  • Property preferences and search history on our platform
  • Payment and transaction information for property purchases or rentals
  • Profile information and preferences you choose to share with us

Usage Data

We track how you interact with our website to understand user behavior and improve our services. This includes information about your device, browser, pages visited, and the time spent on our platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process transactions, communicate with you about your account, and send you updates about new properties and services that match your interests.

Primary Uses

  1. Processing your property searches and transaction requests
  2. Sending you notifications about new listings matching your criteria
  3. Communicating important updates and policy changes
  4. Providing customer support and resolving your inquiries

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only when necessary with trusted partners and service providers who help us deliver our services. We maintain strict agreements to ensure your data remains protected and confidential.

We Never Sell Your Data

Your privacy is our priority. We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures and follow best practices to ensure your data is protected from unauthorized access and maintained with the highest standards of confidentiality.

Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using advanced security protocols to prevent interception.

Secure Storage

Your data is stored in secure facilities with restricted access, regular backups, and comprehensive disaster recovery procedures in place.

Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal data. You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by contacting our privacy team.

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you at any time.

Right to Rectification

If your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections or updates to your data.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data from our systems, subject to certain legal obligations and exceptions.
